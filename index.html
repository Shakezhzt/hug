About Water Source Protection

Water source protection focuses on safeguarding rivers, lakes, reservoirs, and groundwater from pollution and overuse. Clean and safe water sources are essential for human health, ecosystems, and economic development.

Why It Matters

  • Ensures safe drinking water for communities
  • Protects aquatic ecosystems and biodiversity
  • Reduces water treatment costs
  • Supports sustainable agriculture and industry

Key Protection Measures

  • Controlling industrial and agricultural pollution
  • Establishing water protection zones
  • Monitoring water quality regularly
  • Promoting water conservation and reuse
  • Raising public awareness and education

How You Can Help

Everyone plays a role in protecting water sources. Simple actions such as reducing waste, using eco-friendly products, conserving water, and supporting environmental initiatives can make a significant difference.
